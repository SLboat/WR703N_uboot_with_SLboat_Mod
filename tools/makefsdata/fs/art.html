ART UPGRADE

You are going to upload new ART (Atheros Radio Test) image for the router. Please, choose appropriate file from your local hard drive and click Upload . The file will be downloaded, stored in RAM and then copied to FLASH (last 64 KB ). You can also upgrade U-Boot and firmware .

WARNINGS
  • do not power off the device during upload
  • if everything goes well, the router will restart
  • in case of any error during or after image upload, LED(s) will blink fast for a while and then the web failsafe mode will restart
  • you can upload whatever you want, so be sure that you choose proper ART image for your router!
  • you should upload a file with size of exactly 64 KB
